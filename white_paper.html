Thesis

Every task performed day to day inside your business requires brain power. Some tasks require lots. Some don't require much. Nevertheless, every task requires some level of thought or reasoning.

Completing tasks, and the thought required to do it takes time, and time costs money. At the start of each business day a timer starts. Each second that goes by money is spent to keep things moving. We've decided to fix this.
