What we do


We're an AI consultancy that reformulate business processes as text reasoning problems. We offer consultancy and deployment of AI on a subscription basis. Our products serve as virtual brains, capable of generating content, understanding data, and making decisions in real-time.

We distill your current processes until we are left with a binary decision tree. The binary decision tree represents all of your current processes in their most detailed form. This map can be used as a roadmap to automate your business.
